National Emblem Image Requirements

The file "national_emblem.png" should be replaced with an official, high-resolution image of the national emblem when deploying the application. 

The image should:
- Be in PNG format with transparency
- Have dimensions of approximately 300x300 pixels
- Be properly licensed for use in a government application
- Follow all official guidelines for display of national symbols

For official government applications, obtain the authorized emblem image from the appropriate government department.
